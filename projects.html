1. Rain Water Harvesting (Project made at school level)

The main purpose of rainwater harvesting is to receive as much as you can rainwater and store it in some place in order to use it later.Collecting a lot of rainwater in the tank and creating a plan how to use it later helps us a lot. This type of project has low economic and ecological attractions from govt. and people who care about the earth.

The benefits of water recovery for rain harvesting project:a lot of water savings especially if you have a home in the area where the season is hot you can use for your garden too.

    The recovered rainwater can be used for:
  • washing home
  • Wash your vehicle
  • water the plants in the garden
  • small swimming pool
  • water for toilets.
